Complete Denture

What is a Complete Denture?

A complete denture is a removable appliance used to replace all missing teeth in the upper or lower arch.

Why are Complete Dentures Important?

  • Restores the function and aesthetics of missing teeth.
  • Improves speech and ability to chew.
  • Supports facial muscles and improves appearance.

What to Expect with Complete Dentures?

  • Consultation: The dentist assesses your gums and bone structure.
  • Impression: An impression is taken to create a custom fit.
  • Fitting: Dentures are adjusted for comfort and function.

Benefits of Complete Dentures

  • Restores your ability to chew and speak properly.
  • Enhances your smile and appearance.
  • Custom fit ensures comfort and ease of use.
