Fixed Bridge

What is a Fixed Bridge?

A fixed bridge is a dental prosthesis used to replace one or more missing teeth by anchoring to adjacent teeth. It restores both function and aesthetics to your smile.

Why is a Fixed Bridge Important?

  • Restores your ability to chew and speak properly.
  • Maintains the shape of your face and smile.
  • Prevents the shifting of surrounding teeth.

What to Expect with a Fixed Bridge?

  • Consultation: The dentist evaluates your teeth and discusses the procedure.
  • Preparation: Adjacent teeth are prepared for crowns to support the bridge.
  • Impression: A mold is taken to create the custom bridge.
  • Placement: Once ready, the bridge is secured in place for a comfortable fit.

Benefits of a Fixed Bridge

  • Durable and functional replacement for missing teeth.
  • Natural appearance that enhances your smile.
  • Improves oral health by maintaining tooth alignment.
