Tooth Whitening

What is Tooth Whitening?

Tooth whitening is a cosmetic procedure that lightens the color of teeth by removing stains and discoloration, giving you a brighter smile.

Why is Tooth Whitening Important?

  • Removes stains caused by food, drink, and smoking.
  • Improves the appearance of your smile.
  • Boosts self-confidence with a brighter smile.

What to Expect with Tooth Whitening?

  • Consultation: The dentist assesses the condition of your teeth and recommends the best whitening option.
  • Procedure: Whitening agents are applied to your teeth to remove surface stains.
  • Results: Teeth will appear several shades whiter after the procedure.

Benefits of Tooth Whitening

  • Removes stubborn stains and discoloration.
  • Improves smile aesthetics and self-confidence.
  • Safe and effective professional treatment.
