Oral Prophylaxis / Cleaning

What is Oral Prophylaxis?

Oral prophylaxis, or dental cleaning, removes plaque, tartar, and stains from your teeth to maintain good oral hygiene and prevent gum disease.

Why is Oral Prophylaxis Important?

  • Helps prevent cavities and gum disease.
  • Improves overall oral health and appearance.
  • Removes hard-to-reach plaque and tartar.

What to Expect During Oral Prophylaxis?

The procedure involves removing plaque and tartar buildup on the teeth, polishing the teeth, and cleaning hard-to-reach areas.

  • Scaling: Plaque and tartar are removed using a special tool.
  • Polishing: The teeth are polished to remove surface stains.
  • Fluoride treatment: Optional for extra protection.

Benefits of Oral Prophylaxis

  • Prevents cavities and gum disease.
  • Freshens breath and enhances smile appearance.
  • Promotes overall oral health and hygiene.
