Pit and Fissure Sealant

What is a Pit and Fissure Sealant?

Pit and fissure sealants are protective coatings applied to the chewing surfaces of the back teeth to prevent cavities.

Why are Pit and Fissure Sealants Important?

  • Protects hard-to-clean areas from decay.
  • Creates a barrier against bacteria and food particles.
  • Ideal for children and teens with developing teeth.

What to Expect with Pit and Fissure Sealants?

  • Application: The dentist cleans the tooth and applies the sealant.
  • Quick and painless: The procedure is fast and non-invasive.
  • Durability: Sealants can last several years with proper care.

Benefits of Pit and Fissure Sealants

  • Prevents cavities in molars and premolars.
  • Reduces the risk of dental decay in children.
  • Cost-effective preventive measure.
