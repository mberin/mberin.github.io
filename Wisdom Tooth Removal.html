Wisdom Tooth Removal

What is Wisdom Tooth Removal?

Wisdom tooth removal is a common procedure to extract the third molars, which often cause pain, infection, or misalignment if left untreated.

Why is Wisdom Tooth Removal Important?

  • Prevents overcrowding and misalignment of teeth.
  • Reduces the risk of infection or decay.
  • Ensures long-term oral health and comfort.

What to Expect During Wisdom Tooth Removal?

The dentist will extract the wisdom teeth under local anesthesia, and you’ll receive post-operative care instructions to ensure proper healing.

  • Anesthesia: The dentist will numb the area around the wisdom tooth.
  • Extraction: The wisdom tooth is carefully removed.
  • Post-care: Healing instructions will be provided to prevent complications.

Benefits of Wisdom Tooth Removal

  • Prevents dental issues like overcrowding and pain.
  • Reduces the risk of infection and gum disease.
  • Improves overall oral health and alignment.
