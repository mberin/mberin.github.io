Hawley Retainer

What is a Hawley Retainer?

A Hawley retainer is a removable device used after braces to maintain the alignment of your teeth. It consists of a metal wire that surrounds your teeth and keeps them in position.

Why is a Hawley Retainer Important?

  • Maintains teeth alignment after braces.
  • Prevents teeth from shifting back to their original position.
  • Durable and easy to adjust if necessary.

What to Expect with a Hawley Retainer?

  • Fitting: The retainer is custom-made for your teeth.
  • Usage: It must be worn as instructed by the dentist, typically full-time at first and then only at night.
  • Maintenance: The retainer should be cleaned regularly.

Benefits of a Hawley Retainer

  • Helps keep your teeth in their new positions after braces.
  • Durable and long-lasting.
  • Easy to clean and maintain.
