Partial Denture

What is a Partial Denture?

A partial denture is a removable dental appliance used to replace a few missing teeth in an arch.

Why are Partial Dentures Important?

  • Fills the gaps caused by missing teeth.
  • Improves chewing ability and speech.
  • Prevents shifting of surrounding teeth.

What to Expect with Partial Dentures?

  • Consultation: The dentist evaluates the area of missing teeth.
  • Impression: An impression is taken for a custom fit.
  • Fitting: The partial denture is adjusted for comfort and ease of use.

Benefits of Partial Dentures

  • Restores function and aesthetics.
  • Prevents teeth from drifting into gaps.
  • Removable and easy to clean.
