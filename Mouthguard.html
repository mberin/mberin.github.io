Mouthguard

What is a Mouthguard?

A mouthguard is a protective device worn over the teeth to prevent injury during sports or to manage teeth grinding (bruxism) during sleep.

Why is a Mouthguard Important?

  • Prevents injury to teeth, lips, and gums during sports.
  • Helps protect against grinding damage to teeth.
  • Reduces risk of dental trauma.

What to Expect When Getting a Mouthguard?

  • Consultation: The dentist assesses the need for a custom mouthguard.
  • Impression: An impression of your teeth is taken for a custom fit.
  • Fitting: Once made, the mouthguard is fitted for comfort and protection.

Benefits of a Mouthguard

  • Protects against sports-related injuries.
  • Prevents wear and tear from teeth grinding.
  • Custom fit ensures comfort and effectiveness.
