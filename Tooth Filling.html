Tooth Filling

What is a Tooth Filling?

A tooth filling is a restorative treatment used to repair cavities or damaged teeth by filling in the decayed area.

Why are Tooth Fillings Important?

  • Restores the tooth’s function and integrity.
  • Prevents further decay.
  • Durable materials used for long-lasting results.

What to Expect During a Tooth Filling?

During the procedure, the dentist will remove the decayed portion of the tooth and fill the area with a composite or amalgam material.

  • Anesthesia: The dentist will numb the area around the tooth.
  • Cleaning: The decayed part of the tooth is removed.
  • Filling: The filling material is applied to restore the tooth’s shape.

Benefits of Tooth Fillings

  • Restores tooth function and appearance.
  • Prevents further decay or infection.
  • Provides long-lasting protection for the tooth.
