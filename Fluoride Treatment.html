Fluoride Treatment

What is Fluoride Treatment?

Fluoride treatment is a preventive procedure that helps strengthen teeth, making them more resistant to decay. Fluoride is applied directly to the teeth in the form of gel, foam, or varnish.

Why is Fluoride Treatment Important?

  • Strengthens enamel and helps prevent cavities.
  • Especially beneficial for children, but adults can also benefit.
  • Reduces the risk of tooth decay.

What to Expect with Fluoride Treatment?

  • Application: The dentist applies fluoride to the teeth for a few minutes.
  • Painless: The treatment is quick and pain-free.
  • Effectiveness: You should avoid eating or drinking for at least 30 minutes after the treatment to let the fluoride fully absorb.

Benefits of Fluoride Treatment

  • Helps prevent tooth decay in children and adults.
  • Strengthens teeth by remineralizing enamel.
  • Non-invasive and painless.
