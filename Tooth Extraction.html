Tooth Extraction

What is Tooth Extraction?

Tooth extraction is a procedure to remove a tooth that is damaged, decayed, or causing overcrowding in the mouth.

Why is Tooth Extraction Important?

  • Necessary for impacted or damaged teeth.
  • Helps relieve pain and prevent infection.
  • Prepares the mouth for orthodontic treatment if needed.

What to Expect During Tooth Extraction?

The tooth will be carefully removed after the area is numbed, and you’ll receive care instructions to ensure proper healing.

  • Anesthesia: The dentist will numb the area around the tooth.
  • Extraction: The tooth is gently removed.
  • Post-care: Instructions will be provided for healing.

Benefits of Tooth Extraction

  • Eliminates pain and infection.
  • Prevents overcrowding and alignment issues.
  • Improves overall oral health.
