Root Canal Treatment

What is Root Canal Treatment?

Root canal treatment involves removing infected or damaged tissue from inside the tooth, disinfecting the root canal, and sealing it to prevent further infection.

Why is Root Canal Treatment Important?

  • Relieves severe tooth pain.
  • Prevents the spread of infection.
  • Saves the natural tooth from extraction.

What to Expect During Root Canal Treatment?

  • Initial Exam: The dentist evaluates the tooth with X-rays.
  • Procedure: Infected tissue is removed, and the root canal is cleaned and sealed.
  • Follow-up: A crown may be placed to protect the treated tooth.

Benefits of Root Canal Treatment

  • Eliminates pain and infection.
  • Preserves your natural tooth.
  • Restores functionality and appearance.
