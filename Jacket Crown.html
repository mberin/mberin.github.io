Jacket Crown

What is a Jacket Crown?

A jacket crown is a custom-made cap that covers a damaged or decayed tooth, restoring its shape, size, and strength. It is designed to look and function like a natural tooth.

Why is a Jacket Crown Important?

  • Protects and strengthens damaged teeth.
  • Improves the appearance of discolored or misshaped teeth.
  • Helps maintain proper bite and alignment.

What to Expect with a Jacket Crown?

  • Consultation: The dentist will assess your tooth and discuss the best options.
  • Preparation: The tooth is shaped to fit the crown.
  • Impression: A mold of your tooth is taken for the custom crown.
  • Placement: Once ready, the crown is placed and adjusted for comfort.

Benefits of a Jacket Crown

  • Durable and long-lasting solution for damaged teeth.
  • Natural appearance that blends with your smile.
  • Restores functionality, allowing you to eat and speak comfortably.
